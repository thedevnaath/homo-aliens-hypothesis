About the Hypothesis

The Cosmo Sapiens Hypothesis proposes that Homo sapiens may represent a deliberate genomic re-engineering performed by an advanced non-Earth lineage. This paper outlines a theoretical framework involving genomic adaptation, neurodevelopmental scaling, and engineered consciousness.
